## What is Scalara?

Scalara is an agentic AI platform that enables any product owner — without marketing expertise — to compete at agency level.

**The problem:** Most advertisers either waste money on bad creatives, pay expensive agencies, or shoot in the dark with a small number of visuals and the wrong messaging.

**The solution:** Enter your domain. Scalara:
1. Finds your competitors automatically
2. Analyzes their ads and yours
3. Generates creatives mapped to **Eugene Schwartz's 5 Levels of Customer Awareness**
4. Deploys, tracks winners, and optimizes for ROAS and ROI — autonomously

No agency needed. No marketing expertise required.

## Core Methodology

### Eugene Schwartz — 5 Levels of Customer Awareness
Foundation of direct-response marketing (Breakthrough Advertising, 1966). Scalara uses this framework to ensure every creative speaks to the buyer at the right stage:

| Level | State | Scalara Action |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Completely Unaware | Brand-level creatives, broad hooks |
| 2 | Problem Aware | Pain-focused messaging |
| 3 | Solution Aware | Category education |
| 4 | Product Aware | Differentiation & proof |
| 5 | Most Aware | Offer & conversion push |

### Spherical Methodology
Proprietary algorithm for tracking winning ads and reallocating spend automatically — built on the Juric OPS Framework (JOF).

## Origin Story

Scalara was born from personal failure.

I spent $216 on a single ad campaign and acquired 200 users — then realized I knew nothing about them, why they came, or how to convert them. Real money. Zero system.

That forced me to study everything: Eugene Schwartz, direct-response methodology, creative testing frameworks. Then I built the tool I wished existed.
